Quick answers for quick decisions

What is Fraction Billing used for? +
Our platform streamlines medical billing workflows by offering credentialing, claims submission, coding, and reimbursement tracking all in one secure, cloud-based system.
Is Fraction Billing HIPAA-compliant? +
Yes. Every process is built around 100% HIPAA-compliant practices, from data handling to claims transmission.
Can multiple providers use the same account? +
Yes — Growth and Pro plans support multi-provider groups with shared reporting and separate billing profiles.
Can I access the system from mobile devices? +
Yes, the practice analytics dashboard is fully responsive, so you can check collections and claim status from any device.
Do you offer training or onboarding support? +
Every new practice gets a guided onboarding session and ongoing training as our tools and your team evolve.
What kind of customer support do you offer? +
24/7 support with a real response time — underserved practices can't afford to wait on hold.
